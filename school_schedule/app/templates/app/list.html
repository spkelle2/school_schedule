Welcome to School Schedules! Click the Excel link to download the form.

Once open, move the x's in the "Availabilities" sheet to the times each of your students are available as well as when you would like to have planning period and lunch. Then on the "Requirements" sheet, specify how many periods you must meet with each student, plan, or eat.

Feel free to add or remove students and/or times on the "Availabilities" sheet by changing the number of filled in rows or columns. If you change the number of students, be sure to add/remove them and the number of times you must meet with them on the "Requirements" sheet.

Once you are finished, save the excel form. Select it to upload via the "Choose File" button below. Once you choose it, you can hit "Upload" to send it to the scheduler. The scheduler will automatically have your computer download the solution when it is done. Open the returned excel file to the "Solution" sheet to find your schedule solved!
